Did you feel it?

2011 seismic activity - A worldwide review.

The year 2011 registered 2694 earthquakes with magnitudes higher than 5, including two of the costliest earthquakes in history, the 9.1 Tōhoku earthquake in Japan (Property damage: $360 billion) and the 6.3 Christchurch earthquake in New Zealand ($15–40 billion).

An interesting fact is that, out of the 2694 earthquakes, only 207 had magnitudes higher than 6 and an alarming 20 had magnitudes higher than 7. The 9.1 Tōhoku earthquake was the "largest quake recorded in Japan and the fourth largest in the world since seismic recording".

Join me on the early stages of my project (ETL and visualization only) that aims to model and classify countries' preparedness to cope with natural disasters.
